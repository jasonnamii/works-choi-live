02 · SUPERPOSITION

03 · MEASUREMENT

측정

자연 전체가 한순간에 붕괴하는 장면은, 관측자가 전체 관계망에서 자신과 연결된 조건부 단면을 하나의 기록으로 갖는 장면으로 바뀝니다.

현재의 경계. 세 설명은 BCM의 핵심 가설이 제공하는 일관된 재해석이지, 아직 수학적으로 완결된 도출이 아닙니다. 물리이론이 되려면 측정 설정별 공동확률 $P(a,b\mid x,y)$를 연결 구조에서 계산하고 표준 양자역학의 결과를 회복해야 합니다.
06 · THE OBJECTION

가장 강한 반론

“그건 설명이 아니라
이름을 바꾼 것 아닌가?”

현재 단계에서는 유효한 반론입니다.

‘연결’이라는 말을 붙이는 것만으로 새 물리이론이 되지는 않습니다. BCM이 이론이 되려면 하나의 연결연산자가 관측자의 시간·동역학·확률을 실제로 생성해야 합니다.

THE BCM RESEARCH TARGET
$$(H_O,\tau_O,P_O)=\mathfrak F(A,\Pi_O)$$

연결 구조 $A$와 관측자 절단 $\Pi_O$가 주어졌을 때, 그 관측자가 경험할 유효 동역학 $H_O$, 시간 순서 $\tau_O$, 관측확률 $P_O$가 계산되어야 합니다.

01 · WELL-POSED연결연산자의 공간·대칭·수렴 조건을 정의한다.
02 · RECOVERY표준 양자역학의 확률과 무신호 조건을 회복한다.
03 · DIFFERENCE기존 이론과 다른 정량적 보정 하나를 도출한다.
04 · FALSIFICATION그 보정을 반증할 수 있는 실험을 지정한다.

우리는 자연을 시간 속에서 변하는 사물들의 집합으로 보았습니다. 그래서 양자 얽힘을 볼 때마다 무엇이 얼마나 빨리 이동했는지를 물었습니다.

그러나 관계가 사물보다 먼저라면 질문은 달라집니다.

무엇이 이동했는가가 아니라, 왜 하나의 연결을 두 사건으로 경험하는가?

우리는 시간이 흐르는 세계에 사는 것이 아니라,
연결된 세계를 시간으로 읽고 있는지도 모릅니다.

Barbourian Connectivity Model · Concept Hypothesis

No time.Only connections.

Nothing in quantum entanglement travels faster than light.
If BCM is right, that is because nothing travels at all.

HYPOTHESIS · A NEW STARTING POINT, NOT A COMPLETED THEORY
SCROLL TO INVERT THE QUESTION
01 · PREMISE

The question
we asked wrong

“At the instant one particle is measured, how does information reach the other?”

This is the question we almost automatically ask about entanglement. We picture two independently existing particles and imagine that a change at one location must somehow travel to the other.

But the question already assumes four things that the experiment itself does not tell us.

ASSUMPTION 01

Objects exist before relations.

ASSUMPTION 02

Spatially distant particles are fundamentally separate.

ASSUMPTION 03

Correlation requires something to travel.

ASSUMPTION 04

That travel must occur inside time.

If relation comes before object,
the need for anything to travel disappears.

02 · INVERSION

One inversion

OBJECT-FIRST WORLD

A and B exist first. Their relation happens later, between them.

A
B
What moved, and how fast?

Objects come first.
Connection is an event.

RELATION-FIRST WORLD · BCM

One relation exists first. A and B are the two ends at which it appears.

A
B
This whole is one relation

Connection comes first.
Connection exists.

BCM hypothesizes that nature is not a collection of objects changing in time, but a static whole of relations that coexist.

In this view, a connection does not appear at a moment or move to another place. It simply exists. What we call particles are endpoints at which that network becomes manifest in a particular observation.

03 · SIMULTANEITY

Must simultaneity
be about time?

$$\operatorname{Sim}(p,q)\iff a_{pq}\neq0$$

Two points are simultaneous not when they share a time coordinate,
but when they belong to one physical connection.

VIEWCRITERIONQUESTION
NewtonThe same absolute timeDo the clocks of the universe show the same value?
EinsteinThe same time in one inertial frameAre they simultaneous for this observer?
BCM hypothesisMembership in one connectionDoes a physical relation exist between them?

Newton and Einstein answered “when is the same?” differently.
BCM changes the prior question.

04 · TWO LAYERS

Nature's connection.
The observer's order.

Connections do not travel. Only signals arriving at an observer travel.

In BCM, a nonlocal connection and a faster-than-light signal are not the same claim. A connection at the natural layer is a static relation. A signal at the observer layer is a local physical record constrained by distance, medium, noise, and the speed of light.

NATURE

Connection

A static relation between points or subsystems. It does not travel and is not a controllable message channel.

OBSERVER

Signal

A physical trace that arrives at an observer, constrained by light speed, distance, medium, delay, and noise.

INTERPRETATION

Information

The reduction of an observer's uncertainty through a signal. It is not identical to the connection itself.

Nature has relations.
For an observer, order appears.

05 · CONSEQUENCES

One inversion.
Three phenomena change.

01 · ENTANGLEMENT

Entanglement

The image of two distant particles exerting instantaneous influence becomes the image of one nonseparable relation appearing in two observations. Not transfer, but joint structure.

02 · SUPERPOSITION

Superposition

The image of one object wavering among realities becomes the coexistence of relational amplitudes not yet conditioned into one observer record.

03 · MEASUREMENT

Measurement

The image of nature collapsing everywhere at once becomes an observer obtaining a conditional slice of the relational whole as a physical record.

Present boundary. These are coherent reinterpretations supplied by the BCM hypothesis, not completed mathematical derivations. A physical theory must calculate setting-dependent joint probabilities $P(a,b\mid x,y)$ from the connectivity structure and recover the quantitative predictions of quantum mechanics.
06 · THE OBJECTION

The strongest objection

“Is this an explanation—
or only a change of words?”

At the present stage, that objection is valid.

Calling something a connection does not make a new physical theory. BCM becomes one only if a single connectivity operator actually generates an observer's time, dynamics, and probabilities.

THE BCM RESEARCH TARGET
$$(H_O,\tau_O,P_O)=\mathfrak F(A,\Pi_O)$$

Given a connectivity structure $A$ and an observer cut $\Pi_O$, the observer's effective dynamics $H_O$, temporal order $\tau_O$, and observational probabilities $P_O$ must be calculable.

01 · WELL-POSEDDefine the space, symmetries, and convergence conditions of the connectivity operator.
02 · RECOVERYRecover quantum probabilities and the no-signalling condition.
03 · DIFFERENCEDerive one quantitative correction that standard theory does not predict.
04 · FALSIFICATIONSpecify an experiment capable of refuting that correction.

We have treated nature as a collection of objects changing in time. So whenever we encounter entanglement, we ask what moved and how fast.

But if relation comes before object, the question changes.

Not what travelled, but why one connection is experienced as two events.

Perhaps we do not live in a world through which time flows.
Perhaps we read a connected world as time.

A · TECHNICAL NOTES

Boundary of the hypothesis.
The next question.

ESTABLISHED PHYSICS

Violations of Bell inequalities and quantum no-signalling are experimentally and theoretically established starting points.

BCM HYPOTHESIS

Nature is a static whole of relations, and time is the ordering of relational records available to an observer.

OPEN DERIVATION

The generative law producing probabilities, effective dynamics, and a distinctive prediction from connectivity remains open.

Minimal formal skeleton

The present conceptual core compresses into three expressions.

$$\mathcal G=(V,A),\qquad a_{ij}\in\mathbb C$$
$$\operatorname{Sim}(p,q)\iff a_{pq}\neq0$$
$$(H_O,\tau_O,P_O)=\mathfrak F(A,\Pi_O)$$

The first expression is a static complex relational network, the second is the connection-based simultaneity hypothesis, and the third is the missing generative law. The precise operator conditions on $A$ and the explicit form of $\mathfrak F$ remain future work.

What this document does not claim
  • Faster-than-light information transfer or a controllable nonlocal signal channel
  • A completed new numerical prediction for Bell experiments
  • A completed derivation or unification of the Born rule, measurement, or general relativity
  • The emergence of time or a coherence threshold from graph crossing number alone
  • That signal processing, GPS, or three-body applications verify BCM's ontology
Position relative to prior work

BCM must engage existing work on timeless universes, relational quantum mechanics, internal clocks, and emergent time. Its test of novelty is not their combination, but a concrete law that generates observer physics from a connectivity operator.
