01

Why K-Pop, Why Now?

Your child dances to K-Pop alone in their room. They match choreography in front of the mirror, memorize lyrics of their favorite groups, and film cover videos with friends.

That time is not just playtime.

Right now, your child is exploring — through body and emotion — who they are and what moves them. KISAS Kpop Camp is an experience camp designed so this exploration can happen in Seoul, for 10 days, with space and joy. Turn emotions into songs with AI, sing K-Pop together, and express it through cover dance.

This is not an intensive training camp.
A camp where students lightly experience 3 tracks — AI Lyrics & Composition · K-Pop Choir · Cover Dance —
and complete it all on a Seoul stage as a showcase.

What your child will
experience in 10 days

From arrival to showcase — a 10-day flow where every day carries clear meaning. Swipe right to see the full schedule.

Day 1

Arrival, and meeting your team

  • ✈️ Arrival in Seoul · Dormitory assignment
  • 🎉 Welcome performance
  • 🤝 Team assignment (Creative Check-in)
Day 2~3

Making your own K-Pop with AI

  • 🌱 Morning (English + major-country interpretation): Humanities Seed Talk — "What's your feeling today, in one sentence?" A light opening conversation
  • 🤖 Afternoon: AI Lyrics & Composition Workshop — From one sentence of emotion to a complete K-Pop song with AI (team original)
  • 🎤 Evening: K-Pop Choir Opening · Vocal basics warm-up
Day 4

A day walking through Seoul (parents may join)

  • 📍 K-Pop landmark tour
  • 👘 Korean culture experience (Hanbok · traditional market · Korean cooking)
  • 🏙️ Seoul sightseeing
  • 👨‍👩‍👧 Today, parents are welcome to join
Day 5~6

Learning the cover dance

  • 💃 K-Pop Cover Dance Basics — Song selection · learning the choreography part by part
  • 🧭 Stage Blocking — Formations · positioning · detail work
  • 🎥 A professional film crew captures the process from class to rehearsal (making-of footage)
Day 7

Completing the choir

  • 🎤 K-Pop Choir Completion — Everyone sings together; strong voices naturally take on soloist parts
  • 🎶 Harmony Detail — Part assignments · choir run-through
  • 🌿 Short free-time blocks help your child recover energy
Day 8~9

Polishing the stage

  • ✨ Full run-through with costumes & lights — a true dress rehearsal
  • 🎬 Making-of Filming — on-site interviews · rehearsal highlights
  • 🎯 Final Rehearsal — end-to-end check across Parts 1–5
Day 10

FINAL SHOWCASE

  • Part 1 Making-of video screening (class footage + AI songwriting documentary, casual tone)
  • Part 2 K-Pop Choir (with live soloists)
  • Part 3 K-Pop Cover Dance team performances
  • Part 4 Finale Choir (iconic Korean K-Pop song, performed by everyone)
  • Part 5 Completion ceremony · Creative Spirit Award · Commemorative package

Frequently Asked Questions

The 8 most common questions from parents.

Q1. Is it okay if my child has no K-Pop experience at all?
Yes. From Day 2 we begin with gentle vocal & dance warm-ups. This is an experience-based camp, not intensive training, so children with no prior experience can join comfortably.
Q2. Won't the schedule be too demanding?
Mornings are Seed Talks & AI songwriting workshops · afternoons are cover dance & choir · evenings are self-directed practice, with clearly separated rest periods. Day 4 centers on outdoor activities, and practice from Day 5~9 is spread out so no single day feels too heavy.
Q3. My child doesn't speak Korean at all — is that okay?
Morning Seed Talks & AI songwriting workshops are conducted primarily in English. Korean-language sessions and announcements come with simultaneous interpretation, and major-country language interpretation is provided based on each cohort's national mix.
Q4. Can parents travel to Seoul together?
Yes. Parent accommodation · airfare · tours can be arranged via individual booking or through the All-in-One Package. The All-in-One Package is under review and preparation with per-cohort customized quotations, and once confirmed will be communicated via your agent. Parents can join on Day 4 outdoor activities · Day 10 Showcase · free time.
Q5. How are Halal meals and prayer/worship times handled?
We respect the diverse food cultures and religions across Asia. Halal · vegetarian · and prayer/worship spaces for Islam · Catholicism · Buddhism and more are customized per cohort based on national and religious composition. Details are communicated separately after enrollment.
Q6. Is this a camp where my child can become a "trainee" or "debut"?
No. This camp is not a trainee-training · audition · or debut program. It is an AI songwriting · choir · cover dance experience + self-exploration camp using K-Pop as a medium. During choir sessions, a child who excels may naturally take a solo part, but this is a natural on-site role — not an audition or selection.
Q7. How do we receive the videos and photos?
Delivered on the final day via a QR-code-based digital archive. Includes the camp documentary · full team performance video · and individual highlights, with lifetime access.
Q8. We're worried about copyright. Can we post the cover videos?
In-camp use is for educational purposes; for external publication, we provide guidance on copyright verification.
