Partner Share Checklist

The part people
remember.

CHORUS = the part people remember. Make 2 versions. 8–12 words. Clarity beats rhyme.

Quick Builder.

Pick one frame. Fill the blank. Read aloud. Clap the rhythm. Choose the one that feels true.

FRAME I — Say my name — _________ (because _________).
FRAME II — When you say my name, I feel _________.
FRAME III — My name is my story — _________.
FRAME IV — You see me when you _________ (say it right).
