04 — PARTNER SHARE

Practice saying it out loud.

Use your identity map to prepare a short introduction. Review the checklist. After you share, fill in what you learned.

✓Partner Share Checklist

I shared 3 parts of my identity map with my partner.
I explained why one part of my identity matters to me.
I asked a question — one open-ended question about their map.
I listened respectfully and took at least one note.
ONE THING I LEARNED
ONE QUESTION I HAVE

⏱30–45 Sec Introduction Planner

Aim for honesty over perfection. The goal is to sound real, not polished.

My name is
One important part of who I am is
This matters because
One thing I hope to grow during camp is

AUTHENTIC VOICE — Sound real, not perfect. Use your own examples. Choose words you would actually say. Speak clearly, slowly, and with confidence.

05 — CHORUS LAB

The part people remember.

CHORUS = the part people remember. Make 2 versions. 8–12 words. Clarity beats rhyme.

Quick CHORUS
Builder.

Pick one frame. Fill the blank. Read aloud. Clap the rhythm. Choose the one that feels true.

FRAME 1 — Say my name — _________ (because _________).
FRAME 2 — When you say my name, I feel _________.
FRAME 3 — My name is my story — _________.
FRAME 4 — You see me when you _________ (say it right).
