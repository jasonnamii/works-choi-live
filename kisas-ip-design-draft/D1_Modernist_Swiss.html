§ 04 — PARTNER SHARE

Practice saying it out loud.

Use your identity map to prepare a short introduction. Review the checklist below. After you share, fill in what you learned.

FIG. DPartner Share Checklist

I shared 3 parts of my identity map with my partner.
I explained why one part of my identity matters to me.
I asked a question — one open-ended question about their map.
I listened respectfully and took at least one note.
⊕ ONE THING I LEARNED
⊕ ONE QUESTION I HAVE

FIG. E30–45 Sec Introduction Planner

FIG. 03 · AUTHENTIC VOICE

Sound real, not perfect. Use your own examples. Choose words you would actually say. Speak clearly, slowly, and with confidence.

§ 05 — CHORUS LAB

The part people remember.

CHORUS = the part people remember. Make 2 versions. 8–12 words. Clarity beats rhyme.

M / 05 — QUICK BUILDER

Build your chorus.

Pick one frame. Fill the blank. Read aloud. Clap the rhythm. Choose the one that feels true.

FRAME 01Say my name — _________ (because _________).
FRAME 02When you say my name, I feel _________.
FRAME 03My name is my story — _________.
FRAME 04You see me when you _________ (say it right).
